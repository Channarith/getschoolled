1. LOGO
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Canonical brand mark: the Salareen Bayon Buddy - a friendly, secular Khmer-
inspired stone character holding a dark golden medallion stamped with a capital
"S" and a green bodhi leaf. This single character IS the brand, logo, and app
icon: it is the favicon, the nav badge, the apple-touch / app icon, and the
hero/share image. Master art (transparent, full figure):
    apps/web/public/bayon-mark.webp
The square web icons (favicon.ico, logo-mark.webp, logo.webp, icon.png) are
rendered from that master onto a navy rounded square by
scripts/build_bayon_icons.py - do not hand-edit them.

Legacy monogram (kept for the wordmark / 1-color print only): a thin "S" inside
a circular seal crowned by a bodhi leaf (docs/brand/aiclassroom_mark.svg,
apps/web/public/logo-mark.svg). It is no longer used as the favicon or app icon.

Salareen study buddy (product mascot):
  Concept: a friendly, SECULAR character - a calm, gently-smiling face in the
  spirit of classical Khmer (Bayon) craftsmanship, drawn as a modern mascot
  rather than a monument. Its silhouette forms a capital "S" for Salareen, and
  beside it grows a stylized "leaf of knowledge": a heart-shaped, bodhi-style
  leaf whose veins double as a learning/circuit network. It signals curiosity,
  growth, and lifelong learning - explicitly NOT religion.
  Files:
    docs/brand/salareen_bayon_buddy_mascot.png     mascot, color on navy
    apps/web/public/salareen-mascot.webp           web-optimized mascot
    docs/brand/salareen_platform_ecosystem.png     sub-app ecosystem poster
    apps/web/public/salareen-ecosystem.webp        web-optimized poster

Locale mascots (27 languages):
  Each supported language has its own mascot that differs in BOTH colour and
  physique. A per-locale stone tint (hue/saturation/brightness/warmth) is layered
  on top of a per-locale base carving with a slightly different build and arm/leg
  placement (pose) - e.g. sw is a broad grounded kneel, ja a lean upright kneel,
  hi a cross-legged seat, ru a heavy seated build. Every variant keeps the same
  serene face, Khmer lotus crown, and the S-with-bodhi-leaf medallion.
  Build/pose + tint live in packages/shared/src/aoep_shared/mascots.py.
  Pipeline:
    scripts/build_mascot_bases.py        reference-guided raws -> transparent
                                         apps/web/public/mascots/base/{locale}.webp
                                         (km base = the canonical master)
    scripts/generate_locale_mascots.py   tint each base -> web + mobile
                                         {locale}.webp + mobile asset manifest
  Contact sheet: docs/screens/mascots_locale_variants.webp

Mascot/design guardrail:
  - Keep brand and theme art SECULAR. The Salareen buddy is a culturally-
    inspired *character* presented as a friendly study companion - never a
    temple, monument, or devotional object; the leaf is a symbol of knowledge
    and growth, not faith. Keep it respectful and never appropriative.
  - For Khmer-language or Cambodian audience designs, lean on neutral language,
    family, study, food, music, pattern, landscape, or everyday-learning motifs.

2. USAGE
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Keep clear space around the mark equal to the width of the tassel curl.
  - Don't recolor the mark beyond the two brand neutrals (navy / light) or a
    single accent color; use the binary marks for 1-color contexts.
  - Don't add gradients, drop shadows, or 3-D effects.
  - Minimum legible size: ~16px (favicon) for the mark, ~120px wide for the
    wordmark.

4. TRADEMARK: "AI Classroom"  (NOT legal advice - consult a TM attorney)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
From web search of USPTO-style records (not an authoritative clearance search):
  - No exact LIVE U.S. federal registration was found for the wordmark
    "AI CLASSROOM" in education/software classes.
  - Related/nearby marks show how crowded + hard-to-register this space is:
      * "LEARN WITH AI" (Serial 99018704, TIMEBACK LLC) - FINAL REFUSAL.
      * "INTERNATIONAL INSTITUTE OF AI EDUCATION" (99197318) - ABANDONED.
      * "AIBEP" (99404809) - pending, non-final office action.
  - Multiple businesses already trade under "Classroom AI" / "AI Classroom"-like
    names (apps + sites listed above) -> common-law rights + confusion risk.

Assessment:
  - "AI Classroom" is highly DESCRIPTIVE for an AI-taught classroom product.
    Descriptive marks are weak and typically refused on the Principal Register
    absent acquired distinctiveness (cf. the LEARN WITH AI refusal). Expect an
    uphill USPTO path and limited enforceability.
Recommendations:
  - Engage a trademark attorney for a full clearance search (USPTO TESS + state +
    common-law + WIPO/Madrid) before relying on the name.
  - Consider a distinctive/coined brand (logo + a unique wordmark) that is easier
    to register and defend; keep "AI Classroom" as a descriptive tagline. The
    new minimalist mark (this file) is significantly more registrable than the
    previous illustrated mark and should be filed as a DESIGN MARK.
