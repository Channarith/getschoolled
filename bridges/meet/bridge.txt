GOOGLE MEET / CHAT BRIDGE (phase9)
==================================

A bot that bridges Google Meet (and Chat) media into the LiveKit room consumed
by the teaching brain.

CONTRACT
- Join Meet and bridge audio/video/screen-share <-> LiveKit.
- Integrate Chat for text Q&A routed to the Tutor agent.
- Honor platform ToS and surface recording/retention disclosures.

STATUS
- IMPLEMENTED + TESTED (platform-agnostic): uses the same engine as the Zoom
  bridge (aoep_shared.bridges) for meeting-code parsing, the join->bridge->leave
  lifecycle, track wiring, chat->Tutor routing, and disclosures. Wired into the
  integrations service at POST /bridges/meet/connect (and the shared session
  endpoints). Exercised end-to-end via an in-memory transport.
- REQUIRES TO GO LIVE: a Google Workspace service account (GOOGLE_SA_JSON +
  GOOGLE_WORKSPACE_CUSTOMER) and the Meet automation/media runtime, which are
  not available in this environment. Honor Google Meet's automation/ToS limits.
  Until a MeetMediaTransport is wired in, a real connect fails closed with
  BridgeUnavailable.
