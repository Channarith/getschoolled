Cyber Incident Response Drills
==============================

Containment without destruction — ransomware, breach, and insider scenarios.
Use track_id ``cyber_ir`` or domain ``cybersecurity``.

Module 1 — Isolate to slow spread
---------------------------------
Segment affected hosts; avoid wiping disks before evidence preservation unless
business survival requires it.

Module 2 — Verify before trust
------------------------------
CEO fraud wires and phishing pages look urgent. Out-of-band verification is not
optional.

Module 3 — Communicate facts only
---------------------------------
PR and leadership will ask for answers. Separate confirmed facts from hypotheses.

Module 4 — Foresight — what spreads in 15 minutes
-------------------------------------------------
Map lateral movement paths before declaring containment complete.

Practice scenarios: cyber_* , telecom_* outage variants.
