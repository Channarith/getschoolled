IFR Emergency Procedures
========================

Instrument flying emergencies for sim sessions and instrument proficiency.
Use track_id ``aviation_ifr_advanced`` or domain ``aviation_ifr``.

Module 1 — Trust instruments
----------------------------
In IMC spatial disorientation, instrument scan beats somatogravic illusion.
Aviate first — wings level, safe airspeed.

Module 2 — Partial panel
------------------------
Vacuum or electrical failures degrade attitude reference. Know backup instruments
and when to declare / request vectors.

Module 3 — Icing on approach
----------------------------
Hold, divert, or land — decide before accretion exceeds aircraft capability.
Foresight coach: what would you brief before entering clouds?

Module 4 — Missed approach fuel
-------------------------------
Missed approach at minimums with fuel anxiety is a classic trap. Mental rehearsal
before the approach includes divert airport and fuel reserve.

Practice scenarios: ifr_* , aviation_emergency_landing.
