Aviation Emergency Basics
=========================

Engine-out and emergency landing mental preparation for flight simulation training.
Pairs with the platform Emergency Scenario Coach and Situational Analysis agents.

Lesson 1 — Aviate, Navigate, Communicate
----------------------------------------
The memory aid ANC orders priorities under stress: maintain aircraft control first,
then navigate toward a landing area, then communicate. In a sim, practice saying
your first action aloud before touching controls.

Lesson 2 — Best glide and field selection
-----------------------------------------
At partial or total power loss, establish best glide speed promptly. Scan for
suitable fields within glide range; accept off-airport landing when necessary.
The training agent reveals cues progressively — practice naming what you see.

Lesson 3 — Emergency landing checklist (Cessna-style)
-----------------------------------------------------
Fuel selector BOTH, mixture RICH, ignition BOTH, carb heat ON (if equipped).
Do not fixate on checklist before establishing glide and a landing spot.

Lesson 4 — Mental rehearsal before the event
--------------------------------------------
The Foresight Coach asks: what cues would warn you before failure? What if glide
range is insufficient? Rehearse these while cruise is stable — not only after the emergency.

Lesson 5 — Debrief rubric
-------------------------
Control first. Landing area selected early. Position and intent communicated.
Checklist without fixation. Post-landing hazards anticipated (fuel, egress).

Use scenario_id ``aviation_emergency_landing`` in the training API to run the full drill.
