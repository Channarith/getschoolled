First Responder Training Essentials
===================================

Mass-casualty triage, scene safety, and split-minute prioritization drills.
Use track_id ``first_responder`` or domain ``medical`` in the training API.

Module 1 — Scene safety before heroics
--------------------------------------
Your safety enables patient care. Scan for fire, traffic, violence, and
secondary devices before approaching victims.

Module 2 — START triage mindset
-------------------------------
Immediate life threats: airway, breathing, circulation. The loudest patient may
not be the most urgent. Quiet pediatric patients can be critical.

Module 3 — Communicate early
----------------------------
Assign someone to call EMS with location, counts, and hazards. Reassess as
resources arrive.

Module 4 — Hazmat cue awareness
-------------------------------
Odors, downwind positioning, and symptom clusters may indicate chemical exposure.
Do not drive into hot zones without training.

Practice scenarios: medical_triage_quick, hazmat_* , fire_evacuation.
