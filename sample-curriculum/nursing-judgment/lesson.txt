Nursing Judgment Under Pressure
===============================

Bedside clinical judgment drills for rapid response, falls, and medication safety.
Use track_id ``healthcare_clinical`` or domain ``nursing``.

Module 1 — Patient safety first
-------------------------------
When alarms stack up, return to ABCs and whether the patient can wait 30 seconds
for your full attention.

Module 2 — Escalate early
-------------------------
Rapid response beats code blue when you recognize deterioration trends early.
Name what changed: vitals, mental status, work of breathing.

Module 3 — Medication double-check
----------------------------------
Wrong patient, wrong dose, wrong route — pause when something feels off.
Critical thinking coach asks what evidence supports proceeding.

Module 4 — Family and boundaries
--------------------------------
High emotion at bedside is data, not distraction to ignore — but maintain
professional boundaries and call security when needed.

Practice scenarios: nursing_* , medical_* rapid response variants.
