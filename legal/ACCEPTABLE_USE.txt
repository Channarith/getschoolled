AOEP - ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY  (TEMPLATE - requires legal review)
===============================================================
Version: 1.0   Effective: 2026-06-18

Use the Service lawfully, ethically, and only as permitted by your government,
country, state/province, and institution.

PROHIBITED USES
- Any use that violates applicable law or third-party rights.
- Uploading others' personal/biometric data without a lawful basis/consent.
- Using the Service for surveillance, profiling, or social scoring of students.
- EMOTION RECOGNITION in education where prohibited (e.g. EU AI Act) - the
  platform disables this by region; do not attempt to circumvent it.
- Real-time remote biometric identification where prohibited by law.
- Academic dishonesty, or using AI output to misrepresent authorship.
- Generating unsafe, harassing, hateful, or illegal content; attempting to
  bypass the hallucination guard or safety/HIL controls.
- Scraping or ingesting non-permissively-licensed content (the harvester license
  gate must not be bypassed).
- Reverse engineering, unauthorized access, or removing AI-disclosure / content-
  credential markings.

REGION-AWARE RESTRICTIONS
The in-product Compliance policy (aoep_shared/compliance.py) enforces region
rules (FERPA/COPPA/GDPR/BIPA/EU AI Act). Operating in a region requires meeting
its rules (e.g. written biometric consent in Illinois; parental consent for
under-13; no emotion recognition in the EU).

ENFORCEMENT
Violations may result in feature gating, suspension, or termination, and may be
reported to authorities where required.
