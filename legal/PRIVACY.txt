AOEP - PRIVACY NOTICE  (TEMPLATE - requires legal review)
=========================================================
Version: 1.0   Effective: 2026-06-18

This Notice explains what data the Service processes, why, and your rights. It is
designed to align with FERPA, COPPA (FTC 2025 amendments), GDPR, CCPA/CPRA,
BIPA/CUBI, and applicable state student-privacy laws. Counsel must finalize it.

1. DATA WE PROCESS
   - Account/profile: name, role, institution, language.
   - Learning data: questions, answers, homework submissions, quiz results,
     mastery/adaptivity signals.
   - Optional biometric data (face embeddings) ONLY with consent (see 4).
   - Engagement signals (attention/gaze) where permitted (see 6).
   - Operational logs and security telemetry.

2. WHY (PURPOSES) & MINIMIZATION
   To deliver and personalize instruction, assess learning, ensure safety/
   integrity, and secure the Service. We collect only what is reasonably
   necessary (data minimization). We do NOT sell personal data.

3. STUDENT DATA / FERPA & SCHOOL OFFICIAL EXCEPTION
   When used by a school, AOEP acts as a "school official" / processor under the
   institution's direction and uses student education records solely for the use
   and benefit of the school and for no other commercial purpose. Institutions
   may review, export, correct, and request deletion of student data. See the
   Data Processing Addendum (legal/DPA.txt).

4. CHILDREN (COPPA) & MINORS
   For users under 13 (US) or the applicable local age, we require verifiable
   parental consent or valid school authorization. Per the FTC's 2025 COPPA
   amendments, biometric identifiers are personal information; we apply data
   minimization, separate opt-in before any third-party sharing, retention limits,
   and security safeguards, and we do not condition participation on optional data.

5. AI & AUTOMATED PROCESSING
   AI assists instruction, grading, and (optionally) recognition. We provide AI
   disclosure, citations/groundedness, human-in-the-loop review, and model cards.
   You may request human review of AI decisions that affect you.

6. BIOMETRICS & EMOTION/ENGAGEMENT (region-restricted)
   Face recognition is opt-in and consent-gated, with a name-only fallback;
   embeddings are encrypted, kept within the configured boundary, and deletable.
   In Illinois (BIPA) and similar regimes we obtain written consent and publish a
   retention/destruction schedule. EMOTION RECOGNITION IS DISABLED where prohibited
   (e.g. EU AI Act bans emotion recognition in education); the Compliance policy
   enforces this by region.

7. RETENTION & DELETION
   Data is retained only as long as necessary or as set by your institution's
   retention schedule (retention_days), then deleted/anonymized. You/your
   institution may request deletion at any time.

8. SHARING
   With service providers under contract, with your institution, and as required
   by law. No sale of personal data; no third-party sharing of children's data
   without separate consent.

9. SECURITY & BREACH NOTIFICATION
   We apply technical and organizational safeguards (encryption, access control,
   signed content/webhooks). Material breaches are notified per GDPR Art. 33/34
   and applicable state laws (see SECURITY.txt).

10. YOUR RIGHTS
   Access, correction, deletion, portability, objection/restriction, and consent
   withdrawal (GDPR/CCPA/FERPA as applicable). Contact privacy@example.org.

11. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
   Where data crosses borders, we use lawful transfer mechanisms; deployments can
   be pinned to a region/boundary (local/edge mode) to keep data in-jurisdiction.
